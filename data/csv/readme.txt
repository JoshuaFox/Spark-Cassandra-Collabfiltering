
Columns are 
Col.A: I for training data, V for validation data
Col.B: User ID
Col.C: Employer ID (Product)
Col.D: Rating, generated from Col. E and following
Col.E: Base Rating, either 1 or 5, with odd-numbered and even-numbered users favoring different sets of employers/
Col.F Specifies parity for generating Col.E.
Col.G: Randomization for variability in ratings.
Col.I: Selects +1 or -1 to specify the direction of the random variance.
Col.K: Multiplier to adjust to change the  uniformly distributed random variance generated in Col.F.
